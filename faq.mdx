## Troubleshooting Access Issues

Encounter login problems? Follow these steps to resolve common issues quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache" icon="trash-2">
    Open your browser settings and clear cache and cookies for the last 30 days. Restart the browser and try logging in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email to receive a reset link valid for 1 hour.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Device Time">
    Ensure your device clock matches your time zone. Incorrect time prevents secure connections.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For platform-specific fixes:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Use the latest Chrome or Safari version. Disable ad blockers temporarily, as they may block our scripts.

    ```javascript
    // Check console for errors (F12 > Console)
    console.log("GQ Platform Status: Active");
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS App" icon="apple">
    Force quit the app, then relaunch. Update to the newest version via App Store.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android App" icon="android">
    Clear app cache in Settings > Apps > GQ > Storage. Reinstall if issues persist.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="alert">
  If problems continue, contact support at support@gq.com with your account email and device details.
</Callout>

## Content Update Frequency

GQ publishes fresh content daily to keep you informed on style, culture, and news.

- **Daily**: Style tips, grooming advice, entertainment news
- **Weekly**: In-depth features, sports analysis
- **Monthly**: Full digital magazine issues

New articles appear by 8 AM ET. Check the homepage or subscribe to email alerts for notifications.

<CodeGroup tabs="Email Setup, RSS Feed">
  ```yaml

  Subject: contains "New GQ Content"
  Action: Highlight
  ```
