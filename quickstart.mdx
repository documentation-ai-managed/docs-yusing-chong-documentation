## Overview

Get started with GQ in minutes. Create your account, subscribe for premium access, and dive into expert coverage on style, grooming, fitness, politics, sports, and entertainment. Follow these steps to unlock personalized recommendations and exclusive articles from award-winning writers and top photographers.

<Callout kind="tip">
  New users receive a 7-day free trial on premium content. Cancel anytime.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up using email, Google, or Apple. Choose your preferred method below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web" icon="globe">
    Visit `gq.com/signup` and enter your details.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Email">
        Provide your email address and create a strong password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify">
        Check your inbox for the verification link and click to confirm.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Profile Setup">
        Add your name and preferences for tailored content.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Download the GQ app from the App Store.

    ```bash
    Open App Store → Search "GQ Magazine" → Tap Get
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Install from Google Play.

    ```bash
    Open Play Store → Search "GQ" → Install
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
